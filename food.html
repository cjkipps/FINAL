Desserts

Classic Gingerbread Cookies

There’s nothing quite like the smell of gingerbread baking in the oven to make the house feel festive. These soft, spiced cookies are a holiday favorite for a reason: they’re cozy, comforting, and so much fun to decorate!

Creamy Hot Cocoa

What’s better than sipping a warm cup of hot cocoa on a chilly winter evening? This homemade version is decadently rich and creamy, with a deep chocolate flavor that beats anything from a packet.

White Donut Snowmen

These cute little snowmen made from white donuts are a fun and easy treat to make with kids or for a festive party snack. They’re simple to assemble, yet they’ll steal the show on any dessert table. Plus, they’re delicious! With a little bit of creativity, you can transform store-bought donuts into charming snowmen that are perfect for the season.
